CONTROLS:

CAMERA
Right Click- Moves camera based on what corner of the screen the mouse is in

PLACING/REMOVING TILES
L - switch layers
Left Click - Place/Select Tiles
Q - Delete Tile

COLLISION
C - Toggle collision map visibility
A - Place collision (Must be on first layer)
S - Remove collision (Must be on first layer)

MAP LAYER VISIBILITY
Z - Toggle Layer One Visibility
X - Toggle layer 2 Visibility

T - Save Tilemap
